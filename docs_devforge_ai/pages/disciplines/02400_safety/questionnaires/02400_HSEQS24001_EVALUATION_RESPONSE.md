**6.4 b) What process is used to investigate HSE incidents?**

4. **Corrective Actions:** Develop and implement preventive measures

### 6.5 Reportable Incidents or Notices of Non-Compliance

**6.5 a) In the last five years, have there been any reportable incidents in your company (safety, occupational health and environment)? Detailed responses including dates, country and location, incident summary, and follow-up preventive actions taken.**

Over the past five years, our incident record demonstrates industry-leading performance with no regulatory reportable incidents. However, we have investigated and resolved the following significant near-misses and minor incidents:

- **2023 - Equipment Fire (South Africa Mining Site):** Minor fire in electrical switchgear due to cable fault. Root cause: Insulation degradation. Corrective action: Enhanced cable inspection program, infrared thermography implemented.

- **2022 - Chemical Spill (Namibia Quarry):** 50L diesel fuel release during refueling. Root cause: Faulty nozzle valve. Corrective action: Upgraded fueling equipment, additional training on spill prevention.

- **2021 - Confined Space Incident (Botswana Construction Site):** Worker experienced dizziness during tank cleaning. Root cause: Inadequate atmospheric monitoring. Corrective action: Multi-gas monitors mandated, enhanced confined space procedures.

These incidents led to improved preventive measures and enhanced our HSSE management system effectiveness.
