# Safety Data Pipelines

## Data Sources

- safety management systems
- training platforms
- regulatory databases
- Internal incident reports, risk assessments, training records, compliance data

## Processing Steps

1. Data ingestion from safety management systems
2. Validation and cleansing
3. Transformation and enrichment
4. Storage in Supabase
5. Indexing for search
