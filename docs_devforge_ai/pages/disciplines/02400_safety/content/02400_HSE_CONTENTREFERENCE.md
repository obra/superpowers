## 1. HSE Management Systems

### HSE Policy Statement
**Source**: HSE_Policy_Documents.md, HSE_Manual.md

The organization is committed to maintaining the highest standards of health, safety, and environmental protection in all operations. This commitment includes:

- **Leadership Responsibility**: Executive management provides visible leadership and accountability
- **Employee Participation**: All employees actively participate in HSE activities
- **Continuous Improvement**: Regular review and enhancement of HSE performance
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Adherence to all applicable laws and regulations
- **Risk Management**: Proactive identification and control of workplace hazards

**Source**: HSE_Manual.md

- Tolerability of risk criteria

#### Risk Control Hierarchy
1. **Elimination**: Remove the hazard entirely
2. **Substitution**: Replace with less hazardous alternatives
3. **Engineering Controls**: Physical barriers and ventilation
4. **Administrative Controls**: Procedures and training
5. **Personal Protective Equipment**: Last line of defense
