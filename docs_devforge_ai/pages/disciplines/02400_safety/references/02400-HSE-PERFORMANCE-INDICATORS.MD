# HSE Performance Indicators

## Introduction

This document establishes a comprehensive framework of Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) Performance Indicators for [Company Name]. The framework provides standardized metrics, measurement methods, and reporting procedures to effectively monitor, evaluate, and improve HSE performance across all organizational levels and operational areas.

## Scope of Work

### Purpose
To develop and implement a robust HSE performance measurement system that provides meaningful insights into organizational HSE performance, enables data-driven decision making, and supports continuous improvement initiatives.

### Responsibilities
- **HSE Director**: Overall performance indicator framework development and maintenance
- **Department Managers**: Implementation of performance measurement within their areas
- **HSE Specialists**: Data collection, analysis, and reporting support
- **All Employees**: Participation in performance measurement activities

### Timelines
- Framework development: Ongoing
- Data collection: Continuous real-time and periodic
- Reporting: Monthly, quarterly, and annually
- Review: Semi-annual framework evaluation
- Updates: As business needs evolve

### Deliverables
- Comprehensive HSE performance indicator framework
- Data collection and reporting procedures
- Performance dashboards and scorecards
- Trend analysis and benchmarking reports
- Continuous improvement recommendations

## Performance Indicator Framework

### Indicator Categories
- **Leading Indicators**: Proactive measures predicting future performance
- **Lagging Indicators**: Reactive measures reflecting past performance
- **Monitoring Indicators**: Real-time operational performance measures
- **Outcome Indicators**: Strategic goal achievement measures

### Measurement Principles
- Relevance to organizational HSE objectives
- Measurability and data availability
- Consistency and standardization
- Timeliness and frequency of measurement
- Actionability and improvement potential

### Data Quality Standards
- Accuracy and reliability of data sources
- Consistency in data collection methods
- Completeness of data coverage
- Timeliness of data reporting
- Validation and verification processes

## Leading Indicators

### Proactive Safety Activities
- **Safety Training Completion Rate**: Percentage of required safety training completed
  - Target: 100% completion within required timeframes
  - Measurement: Monthly tracking of training records
  - Responsible: HR and Department Managers

- **Safety Observations Conducted**: Number of safety observations per employee
  - Target: Minimum 2 observations per employee per quarter
  - Measurement: Observation reporting system tracking
  - Responsible: Supervisors and Safety Coordinators

- **Hazard Identification Reports**: Number of hazards reported by employees
  - Target: Continuous improvement in reporting rates
  - Measurement: Hazard reporting system tracking
  - Responsible: All Employees, HSE Coordinators

- **Safety Committee Participation**: Employee participation in safety committees
  - Target: 100% representation from all work areas
  - Measurement: Meeting attendance records
  - Responsible: Safety Committee Chairs

### Risk Management Activities
- **Risk Assessments Completed**: Percentage of required risk assessments completed
  - Target: 100% completion for all operations
  - Measurement: Risk assessment database tracking
  - Responsible: HSE Specialists, Department Managers

- **Control Measures Implementation**: Status of risk control measure implementation
  - Target: 100% implementation within agreed timeframes
  - Measurement: Action tracking system
  - Responsible: Responsible Managers

- **Contractor HSE Performance**: Contractor compliance with HSE requirements
  - Target: 95% compliance rate
  - Measurement: Contractor audit and monitoring results
  - Responsible: Procurement, Project Managers

### Training and Competency
- **Competency Assessments Completed**: Percentage of required competency assessments
  - Target: 100% completion within required intervals
  - Measurement: Competency management system
  - Responsible: HR, Department Managers

- **Training Effectiveness Scores**: Average scores from training evaluations
  - Target: Average score of 4.0 or higher (5-point scale)
  - Measurement: Training evaluation surveys
  - Responsible: Training Department

## Lagging Indicators

### Incident Statistics
- **Total Recordable Injury Rate (TRIR)**: Number of recordable injuries per 200,000 hours worked
  - Target: Industry benchmark or improvement trend
  - Measurement: Incident reporting system
  - Responsible: HSE Department

- **Lost Time Injury Rate (LTIR)**: Number of lost time injuries per 200,000 hours worked
  - Target: Continuous reduction toward zero
  - Measurement: Incident reporting system
  - Responsible: HSE Department

- **Fatality Rate**: Number of fatalities per million hours worked
  - Target: Zero fatalities
  - Measurement: Incident reporting system
  - Responsible: HSE Department

- **Near Miss Reporting Rate**: Number of near miss reports per 1,000 employees
  - Target: Increasing trend indicating improved reporting culture
  - Measurement: Near miss reporting system
  - Responsible: All Employees, HSE Coordinators

### Environmental Incidents
- **Environmental Spill Volume**: Total volume of environmental spills
  - Target: Reduction to zero or regulatory compliance limits
  - Measurement: Environmental incident reporting
  - Responsible: Environmental Specialists

- **Regulatory Non-Compliance Events**: Number of regulatory violations or notices
  - Target: Zero violations
  - Measurement: Compliance monitoring system
  - Responsible: HSE Compliance Team

- **Environmental Fines**: Total monetary value of environmental fines
  - Target: Zero fines
  - Measurement: Financial and compliance records
  - Responsible: HSE Compliance, Finance

### Health Statistics
- **Occupational Illness Rate**: Number of occupational illnesses per 1,000 employees
  - Target: Reduction toward zero
  - Measurement: Medical surveillance records
  - Responsible: Occupational Health, HSE

- **Workers' Compensation Claims**: Number and cost of workers' compensation claims
  - Target: Reduction in frequency and cost
  - Measurement: HR and insurance records
  - Responsible: HR, HSE, Finance

## Monitoring Indicators

### Real-Time Safety Metrics
- **Safety Behavior Compliance**: Percentage compliance with safety behaviors
  - Target: 95% or higher compliance rate
  - Measurement: Safety observation cards
  - Responsible: Supervisors

- **Equipment Inspection Status**: Percentage of equipment inspections completed on schedule
  - Target: 100% completion
  - Measurement: Maintenance management system
  - Responsible: Maintenance Supervisors

- **Permit-to-Work System Usage**: Percentage of high-risk activities using permits
  - Target: 100% compliance
  - Measurement: Permit tracking system
  - Responsible: Operations Supervisors

### Environmental Monitoring
- **Air Quality Measurements**: Compliance with air quality standards
  - Target: 100% compliance with regulatory limits
  - Measurement: Continuous monitoring systems
  - Responsible: Environmental Specialists

- **Water Quality Parameters**: Compliance with water discharge standards
  - Target: 100% compliance with regulatory limits
  - Measurement: Laboratory analysis results
  - Responsible: Environmental Specialists

- **Waste Management Compliance**: Proper waste handling and disposal rates
  - Target: 100% compliance with regulations
  - Measurement: Waste tracking records
  - Responsible: Environmental Coordinators

## Outcome Indicators

### Strategic Performance
- **HSE Strategic Objective Achievement**: Percentage of strategic objectives met
  - Target: 100% achievement of defined objectives
  - Measurement: Strategic plan tracking
  - Responsible: HSE Director, Senior Management

- **Stakeholder Satisfaction**: Satisfaction levels of key stakeholders
  - Target: 85% or higher satisfaction rate
  - Measurement: Stakeholder surveys
  - Responsible: Communications, HSE

- **Regulatory Audit Results**: Performance in regulatory inspections
  - Target: No critical findings, improvement in ratings
  - Measurement: Audit reports and regulatory feedback
  - Responsible: HSE Compliance Team

### Business Impact
- **HSE Return on Investment**: Financial benefits from HSE investments
  - Target: Positive ROI demonstrating value creation
  - Measurement: Cost-benefit analysis
  - Responsible: HSE Director, Finance

- **Insurance Premium Trends**: Trends in insurance costs and coverage
  - Target: Stable or decreasing premiums
  - Measurement: Insurance records and market analysis
  - Responsible: Finance, Risk Management

- **Business Continuity**: Impact of HSE incidents on business operations
  - Target: Minimize operational disruption
  - Measurement: Business impact assessments
  - Responsible: Operations, HSE
