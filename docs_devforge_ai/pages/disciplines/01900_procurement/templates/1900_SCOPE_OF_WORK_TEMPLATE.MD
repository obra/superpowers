# Scope of Work Template

## Document Overview

This Scope of Work (SOW) document template outlines procurement requirements for goods and services in civil engineering projects. It ensures clarity and mutual understanding between all parties involved.

The contractor shall supply the following [goods/services] as per the specifications outlined in Section 3. The [products/services] must be delivered to the designated locations as specified in the logistics section. The contractor is responsible for ensuring that all products meet the required technical specifications and are delivered within the agreed timeline.

## 4. Deliverables and Expected Results

- Complete documentation including safety data sheets (SDS), product specifications, and laboratory analysis certificates
- Delivery of products to the specified locations by the target completion date of [Date]
- Training sessions for personnel on the proper use and application of [products/services]

## 5. Quality Standards and Requirements

All [products/services] must comply with relevant industry standards, including [API, SAE, ISO, etc.] certifications. The contractor must provide evidence of compliance through documentation and testing results.

## 6. Safety and Compliance

The contractor must adhere to all applicable safety regulations and standards. Safety Data Sheets (SDS) must be provided for all [products], detailing handling, storage, and emergency measures.

## 7. Logistics and Transportation

The contractor is responsible for the transportation of [products] to the designated locations as per agreed Incoterm. All shipments must be tracked, and the contractor must provide shipping documentation upon shipment in compliance with Appendix E.

## 8. Packaging and Labelling

All products must be packaged in accordance with industry standards to prevent contamination and damage, in accordance with Appendix F. Labels must include product specifications, handling instructions, and safety information.

## 13. Warranty

- Warranty for all products shall be a minimum of [12] months from the date of delivery.

## 14. Acceptance Criteria and Validation

Acceptance of products will be based on compliance with specifications, correct documentation submitted on time, timely delivery, and satisfactory training completion. Validation will include any inspection at Client discretion, and any testing of samples at Client discretion and costs.

## 15. Responsibilities of All Parties

### Contractor
- Supply products as specified
- Provide documentation
- Conduct training

### Client
- Provide access to delivery locations
- Provide personnel for training if performed on Site

## 16. Risk Management

The contractor must identify potential risks associated with the supply of [products/services] and propose mitigation strategies. This includes ensuring product availability and compliance with safety standards.

## 17. Communication and Reporting Requirements

Regular updates on the status of deliveries and any issues encountered must be communicated to the client. A final report summarizing the project outcomes and compliance with the SOW will be required upon completion.
