# SURFACE FINISHING SPECIFICATION
## Construction Specification Standards - Finishing & Protection

### 1. GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

#### 1.1 Scope
This specification covers the requirements for surface finishing including plastering, painting, tiling, waterproofing, and protective coatings for the construction project.

#### 1.2 References
- SANS 10140: Application of Interior Plaster
- SANS 10141: Application of Exterior Plaster
- SANS 1091: Cementitious Plaster
- SANS 5286: Ready-Mixed Plaster
- SANS 10144: Waterproofing of Buildings

### 2. MATERIALS

#### 2.1 Plaster Materials
**Cementitious Plaster:**
- Portland cement: CEM I 42.5N
- Lime: Hydrated lime (building lime)
- Sand: Clean, well-graded, 0-2.36mm
- Water: Clean, potable water
- Admixtures: Plasticizers and retarders as approved

**Gypsum Plaster:**
- Gypsum hemihydrate (plaster of Paris)
- Lightweight aggregates for reduced density
- Retarders for extended working time
- Water: Clean, potable water

#### 2.2 Paint Materials
**Interior Emulsion Paint:**
- Acrylic emulsion binder
- Titanium dioxide pigment (minimum 15%)
- Extenders: Calcium carbonate, talc
- Additives: Dispersants, thickeners, preservatives
- VOC content: Maximum 30g/L

**Exterior Emulsion Paint:**
- 100% acrylic binder
- Titanium dioxide pigment (minimum 20%)
- Weather-resistant extenders
- UV stabilizers and mildewcides
- VOC content: Maximum 50g/L

#### 2.3 Waterproofing Materials
**Liquid Applied Waterproofing:**
- Acrylic polymer modified cementitious coating
- Two-component polyurethane
- Bituminous emulsion
- Crystalline waterproofing admixture

**Sheet Waterproofing:**
- PVC membranes (1.2mm minimum thickness)
- EPDM membranes (1.0mm minimum thickness)
- Bituminous membranes (3mm minimum thickness)

#### 2.4 Tile Adhesives and Grouts
**Cementitious Adhesive:**
- Portland cement base
- Polymer modification for flexibility
- Sand aggregate (0-1.18mm)
- Workability: 2-3 hours open time

**Epoxy Grout:**
- Two-component epoxy resin
- Pigmented fillers
- Chemical resistant hardeners
- Shore D hardness: 80 minimum

### 3. CONSTRUCTION METHODS

#### 3.1 Plastering Operations
**Surface Preparation:**
- Remove loose material and laitance
- Dampen surface before plastering
- Apply bonding agent if required
- Install reinforcement mesh for external work

**Plaster Application:**
- Scratch coat: 8-10mm thick
- Floating coat: 10-12mm thick
- Finishing coat: 3-5mm thick
- Total thickness: 20-25mm for two-coat work

**Curing Requirements:**
- Keep moist for 7 days minimum
- Protect from direct sunlight and wind
- Temperature: 5°C to 35°C during curing

#### 3.2 Painting Operations
**Surface Preparation:**
- Clean surface of dirt and grease
- Sand smooth existing paint
- Fill cracks and holes
- Apply primer suitable for substrate

**Paint Application:**
- Primer: One coat at 8-10 m²/L
- Undercoat: One coat at 10-12 m²/L
- Finish coat: One or two coats at 12-14 m²/L
- Total DFT: 80-120 microns

**Application Conditions:**
- Temperature: 10°C to 35°C
- Humidity: Maximum 85%
- No application during rain or fog

#### 3.3 Waterproofing Application
**Surface Preparation:**
- Clean and dry substrate
- Remove sharp protrusions
- Fill voids and level surface
- Apply primer if required

**Membrane Application:**
- Ensure full adhesion to substrate
- Overlap seams minimum 100mm
- Seal all penetrations and joints
- Protect membrane during backfilling

#### 3.4 Tiling Installation
**Substrate Preparation:**
- Surface flatness: ±3mm in 2m
- Moisture content: Maximum 3% for wood, 5% for concrete
- pH level: 7-10 for adhesive compatibility

**Adhesive Application:**
- Apply with notched trowel
- Coverage: 3-5 kg/m² depending on tile size
- Open time: 20-30 minutes
- Adjustability time: 15 minutes

### 4. QUALITY CONTROL

#### 4.1 Plaster Quality Control
**Thickness Control:**
- Measure with thickness gauge
- Tolerance: ±2mm of specified thickness
- Frequency: 3 measurements per 10m²

**Surface Regularity:**
- Check with 2m straightedge
- Maximum deviation: 3mm
- Frequency: Every 5m²

**Bond Strength:**
- Pull-off test method
- Minimum strength: 0.5 MPa
- Frequency: One test per 50m²

#### 4.2 Paint Quality Control
**Dry Film Thickness:**
- Measure with magnetic DFT gauge
- Tolerance: ±20 microns of specified
- Frequency: 3 readings per room

**Opacity and Coverage:**
- Visual assessment of hiding power
- No visible substrate through finish
- Touch-up of thin areas

**Adhesion Testing:**
- Cross-cut tape test (ASTM D3359)
- Classification: 0-1 (excellent adhesion)
- Frequency: One test per 100m²

#### 4.3 Waterproofing Quality Control
**Holiday Detection:**
- High voltage spark tester (15kV)
- Frequency: 100% coverage of membrane
- Repair all detected holidays

**Thickness Verification:**
- Measure with thickness gauge
- Tolerance: ±0.2mm of specified
- Frequency: 3 measurements per roll

### 5. TESTING PROCEDURES

#### 5.1 Plaster Testing
**Compressive Strength:**
- Method: SANS 5861-1 (50mm cubes)
- Frequency: One test per 50m³ plaster
- Acceptance: 5 MPa minimum at 28 days

**Surface Hardness:**
- Method: Rebound hammer test
- Acceptance: 15-25 rebound number
- Frequency: One test per 20m²

#### 5.2 Paint Testing
**Viscosity Testing:**
- Method: Stormer viscometer
- Acceptance: Within manufacturer's range
- Frequency: Each batch received

**Fineness of Grind:**
- Method: Hegman gauge
- Acceptance: 5-7 Hegman units
- Frequency: Each batch received

**Dry Time Testing:**
- Method: BK drying time recorder
- Parameters: Touch dry, hard dry times
- Acceptance: Within 2 hours of specification

#### 5.3 Waterproofing Testing
**Water Permeability:**
- Method: SANS 10106 (ponding test)
- Duration: 24 hours minimum
- Acceptance: No water penetration

**Adhesion Testing:**
- Method: Pull-off test
- Minimum strength: 1.0 MPa
- Frequency: One test per 50m²

#### 5.4 Tile Testing
**Adhesive Strength:**
- Method: Pull-off test after 28 days
- Minimum strength: 1.0 MPa
- Frequency: One test per 100m²

**Grout Strength:**
- Method: Compressive strength test
- Minimum strength: 30 MPa
- Frequency: One test per grout batch

### 6. SURFACE FINISHING SYSTEMS

#### 6.1 Interior Plaster Systems
**System 1: Cement Plaster (Two-Coat)**
- Scratch coat: 1:4 cement:sand
- Float coat: 1:3 cement:sand
- Total thickness: 15-20mm
- Finish: Smooth or textured

**System 2: Gypsum Plaster**
- Base coat: Gypsum plaster 10mm
- Finish coat: Skim coat 2mm
- Total thickness: 12mm
- Finish: Smooth only

#### 6.2 Exterior Plaster Systems
**System 1: Cement Plaster**
- Scratch coat: 1:4 cement:sand
- Float coat: 1:3 cement:sand
- Total thickness: 20-25mm
- Finish: Sponge or roughcast

**System 2: Acrylic Render**
- Base coat: Cementitious render
- Finish coat: Acrylic render 2-3mm
- Total thickness: 15-20mm
- Finish: Smooth or textured

### 7. PROTECTIVE COATINGS

#### 7.1 Anti-Corrosion Coatings
**System 1: Zinc-Rich Primer + Epoxy**
- Primer: Zinc-rich epoxy (75 microns)
- Intermediate: Epoxy mastic (125 microns)
- Finish: Polyurethane (50 microns)
- Total DFT: 250 microns

**System 2: Hot-Dip Galvanizing**
- Zinc coating thickness: 85 microns minimum
- Surface preparation: Sa 2.5
- Repair: Zinc-rich paint for damaged areas

#### 7.2 Fire Protection Coatings
**Intumescent Coatings:**
- Base coat: Primer compatible with intumescent
- Intumescent layer: 500-1000 microns
- Finish coat: Decorative topcoat
- Fire rating: 30-120 minutes

### 8. ENVIRONMENTAL CONDITIONS

#### 8.1 Application Conditions
**Temperature Requirements:**
- Plastering: 5°C to 35°C
- Painting: 10°C to 35°C
- Waterproofing: 5°C to 35°C
- Tiling: 5°C to 35°C

**Humidity Limitations:**
- Maximum relative humidity: 85%
- No application during rain or fog
- Surface moisture: Maximum 5% for adhesives

#### 8.2 Curing Conditions
**Plaster Curing:**
- Protect from direct sunlight
- Maintain moist surface for 7 days
- Avoid rapid drying conditions

**Paint Drying:**
- Allow proper ventilation
- Avoid condensation on surface
- Maintain stable temperature

### 9. DEFECTS AND REPAIRS

#### 9.1 Plaster Defects
**Cracking:**
- Hairline cracks: Fill with sealant
- Structural cracks: Cut out and repair
- Prevention: Control joint placement

**Surface Imperfections:**
- Blowholes: Grind and patch
- Sanding marks: Re-skim surface
- Uneven texture: Grind and re-finish

#### 9.2 Paint Defects
**Poor Adhesion:**
- Remove loose paint
- Re-prime surface
- Re-apply paint system

**Color Variation:**
- Check batch numbers
- Intermix paints if compatible
- Touch up with correct shade

#### 9.3 Waterproofing Defects
**Leaks:**
- Locate source of leak
- Remove damaged membrane
- Install new membrane with overlap

**Blistering:**
- Drain blisters and patch
- Identify cause (moisture, entrapped air)
- Apply additional reinforcement

### 10. MEASUREMENT AND PAYMENT

#### 10.1 Surface Finishing Quantities
- Measured in square meters (m²) of finished surface
- Deduct openings >0.5m²
- Include all preparation work

#### 10.2 Payment Items
- Plastering (m²) by type and thickness
- Painting (m²) by system and number of coats
- Waterproofing (m²) by type and thickness
- Tiling (m²) by tile size and type
- Protective coatings (m²) by system
- Testing and inspection (lump sum)
