# EARTHWORKS SPECIFICATION
## Construction Specification Standards - Site Preparation & Earthworks

### 1. GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

#### 1.1 Scope
This specification covers the requirements for all earthworks including excavation, filling, compaction, and site preparation for the construction project.

#### 1.2 References
- SANS 1200 A: Earthworks (General)
- SANS 1200 LB: Earthworks (Large Buildings)
- SANS 1200 LE: Earthworks (Roads and Bridges)
- SANS 1200 LK: Earthworks (Small Buildings)

### 2. MATERIALS

#### 2.1 Fill Materials
**Material Standards:**
- **Select Fill**: Well-graded material with maximum particle size of 37.5mm
- **General Fill**: Material with maximum particle size of 150mm
- **Chemical Requirements**: pH between 5.5 and 9.0, sulfate content < 0.5%
- **Organic Content**: Maximum 1% by dry mass

**Material Sources:**
- On-site excavated material (approved by engineer)
- Imported fill from approved quarries
- Recycled concrete aggregate (maximum 30% by volume)

#### 2.2 Compaction Equipment
- Smooth drum rollers: 8-12 tonne operating weight
- Sheepsfoot rollers: 12-15 tonne operating weight
- Vibratory plate compactors: For trench backfill
- Pneumatic tyred rollers: For finishing operations

### 3. CONSTRUCTION METHODS

#### 3.1 Excavation
**Method 1: Bulk Excavation**
- Use hydraulic excavators with 1.0m³ buckets
- Maintain excavation slopes at 1:1.5 or flatter
- Remove unsuitable material to spoil
- Stockpile suitable material for reuse

**Method 2: Trench Excavation**
- Maximum trench width: 2.0m for services
- Shoring required for depths >1.5m
- Dewatering as required for groundwater

#### 3.2 Filling and Compaction
**Layer Thickness:**
- Select fill: Maximum 200mm loose thickness
- General fill: Maximum 300mm loose thickness

**Compaction Requirements:**
- **Modified AASHTO Density:** Minimum 95% for structural areas
- **Standard AASHTO Density:** Minimum 90% for general areas
- **Field Density Testing:** Nuclear density gauge every 500m²

### 4. QUALITY CONTROL

#### 4.1 Testing Procedures
**Field Density Testing:**
- Method: Nuclear density gauge (ASTM D6938)
- Frequency: One test per 500m² or per layer, whichever is greater
- Acceptance: 95% of modified AASHTO density minimum

**Moisture Content Testing:**
- Method: Oven drying method (SANS 3001-GM7)
- Frequency: Daily or when density tests fail
- Control: ±2% of optimum moisture content

**Particle Size Distribution:**
- Method: Sieve analysis (SANS 3001-GR7)
- Frequency: One test per 1000m³ of material
- Requirements: Within specification grading envelope

#### 4.2 Quality Control Measures
- Daily compaction records
- Equipment calibration checks
- Weather monitoring (no compaction in rain)
- Layer thickness verification
- Surface level surveys

### 5. TESTING PROCEDURES

#### 5.1 Laboratory Testing
**Atterberg Limits:**
- Liquid limit, plastic limit, plasticity index
- Frequency: One test per material source
- Acceptance: Within approved ranges

**Compaction Characteristics:**
- Modified AASHTO compaction test
- Frequency: One test per material type
- Reporting: Optimum moisture content and maximum dry density

#### 5.2 Field Testing
**Nuclear Density Testing:**
- Equipment: Troxler or similar nuclear gauge
- Calibration: Daily calibration check
- Safety: Licensed operator only

**Sand Cone Density Testing:**
- Alternative to nuclear testing
- Method: ASTM D1556
- Use: When nuclear testing not permitted

### 6. ENVIRONMENTAL REQUIREMENTS

#### 6.1 Erosion Control
- Silt fences around site perimeter
- Temporary drainage channels
- Sediment ponds for runoff water
- Stabilized construction entrances

#### 6.2 Dust Control
- Water spraying of exposed surfaces
- Speed limits on site roads (30km/h maximum)
- Wheel washing facilities

### 7. SAFETY REQUIREMENTS

#### 7.1 Excavation Safety
- Daily inspection of excavations
- Protective systems for depths >1.5m
- Access ladders every 6m for trenches
- Spoil piles minimum 1m from excavation edges

#### 7.2 Equipment Safety
- Daily equipment inspections
- Trained operators only
- Backup alarms on mobile equipment
- Emergency stop procedures

### 8. MEASUREMENT AND PAYMENT

#### 8.1 Earthworks Quantities
- Measured in cubic meters (m³) in situ
- Shrinkage factor: 15% for excavated material
- Swell factor: 25% for fill material

#### 8.2 Payment Items
- Bulk excavation (m³)
- Filling and compaction (m³)
- Testing and quality control (lump sum)
- Environmental controls (lump sum)
