# APPENDIX E: LOGISTICS DOCUMENTS SPECIFICATION
## Product/Material Supply - Logistics Documents Specification

### 1. GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

#### 1.1 Scope
This appendix specifies the logistics documentation requirements for the transportation, handling, and delivery of products/materials under this Purchase Order. <!-- data-field="logistics_scope" data-ai-placeholder="LOGISTICS_SCOPE" -->

#### 1.2 Document Standards
All logistics documents must comply with:
- **International transport regulations** (ADR, IMDG, IATA)
- **National road transport legislation**
- **Customs and excise requirements**
- **Company documentation standards**
- **ISO 9001 quality management requirements**

### 2. TRANSPORTATION DOCUMENTS

#### 2.1 Bill of Lading (BOL)
**Required Information:**
- Consignor (supplier) details and contact information
- Consignee (buyer) details and contact information
- Carrier details and vehicle registration
- Date and time of loading
- Origin and destination addresses
- Description of goods (product names, quantities, packaging)
- Gross weight, net weight, and tare weight
- Hazardous goods classification (if applicable)
- Special handling instructions
- Carrier signature and stamp

**Document Format:**
- Original and 3 copies minimum
- Pre-printed company letterhead
- Sequential numbering system
- Digital signature capability

#### 2.2 Consignment Note (Waybill)
**Required Sections:**
1. **Header Information**
   - Consignment number
   - Date and time of dispatch
   - Supplier reference number
   - Purchase order number

2. **Consignor Details**
   - Company name and address
   - Contact person and telephone
   - Tax registration number
   - Emergency contact details

3. **Consignee Details**
   - Delivery address and coordinates
   - Contact person and telephone
   - Receiving hours and procedures
   - Special access requirements

4. **Goods Description**
   - Product codes and descriptions
   - Quantities and units of measure
   - Packaging type and dimensions
   - Gross and net weights
   - Batch numbers and expiry dates

5. **Transportation Details**
   - Vehicle registration and type
   - Driver name and contact details
   - Route information
   - Estimated delivery time

6. **Special Instructions**
   - Temperature requirements
   - Handling precautions
   - Documentation requirements
   - Emergency procedures

#### 2.3 Dangerous Goods Declaration
**Required for Hazardous Materials:**
- Proper shipping name
- UN number and hazard class
- Packing group
- Quantity and type of packaging
- Emergency contact information
- Consignor certification

**Document Retention:**
- Original retained by supplier
- Copy provided to carrier
- Copy retained by consignee
- Minimum retention period: 5 years

### 3. QUALITY AND COMPLIANCE DOCUMENTS

#### 3.1 Certificate of Analysis (COA)
**Required Elements:**
- Product name and code
- Batch number and date of manufacture
- Test results for all specified parameters
- Test methods used
- Acceptance criteria
- Analyst signature and date
- Laboratory accreditation details

**COA Format:**
- Company letterhead with logo
- Clear test result tables
- Pass/fail indicators
- Reference to specifications
- Digital signature capability

#### 3.2 Certificate of Conformity (COC)
**Required Information:**
- Product description and specifications
- Compliance with standards and regulations
- Test results summary
- Quality assurance statement
- Authorized signature
- Date of issue and validity period

#### 3.3 Shelf Life Certification
**Required Details:**
- Product name and batch number
- Date of manufacture
- Shelf life period and conditions
- Storage requirements
- Quality assurance statement
- Authorized signature

### 4. CUSTOMS AND REGULATORY DOCUMENTS

#### 4.1 Commercial Invoice
**Required Information:**
- Invoice number and date
- Supplier and buyer details
- Product descriptions (commercial and technical)
- Quantities, unit prices, and total values
- Incoterms and delivery terms
- Country of origin
- HS codes and customs classifications
- Currency and payment terms

**Supporting Documents:**
- Certificate of origin
- Export declaration (if applicable)
- Import permit (if required)
- Value declaration

#### 4.2 Certificate of Origin
**Types Required:**
- Preferential certificate of origin
- Non-preferential certificate of origin
- EUR.1 certificate (for preferential trade agreements)

**Required Information:**
- Exporter details and signature
- Product descriptions and HS codes
- Country of origin declaration
- Issuing authority stamp and signature
- Date of issue and validity

### 5. DELIVERY AND RECEIPT DOCUMENTS

#### 5.1 Delivery Note
**Required Sections:**
1. **Delivery Information**
   - Delivery note number
   - Purchase order number
   - Delivery date and time
   - Carrier and driver details

2. **Product Details**
   - Product codes and descriptions
   - Delivered quantities
   - Batch numbers and expiry dates
   - Packaging condition

3. **Quality Documentation**
   - Certificate of Analysis reference
   - Safety Data Sheet reference
   - Quality control results

4. **Acceptance Details**
   - Receiver name and signature
   - Date and time of receipt
   - Comments or discrepancies noted
   - Quality inspection results

#### 5.2 Goods Receipt Note (GRN)
**Required Information:**
- GRN number and date
- Purchase order and delivery note references
- Supplier details
- Product descriptions and quantities received
- Quality inspection results
- Discrepancy details (if any)
- Receiver and inspector signatures

**GRN Process:**
1. Physical receipt of goods
2. Quantity verification
3. Quality inspection
4. Documentation check
5. System entry and approval
6. Supplier notification

### 6. TRACEABILITY AND RECORD KEEPING

#### 6.1 Batch Traceability Records
**Required Documentation:**
- Batch manufacturing records
- Raw material certificates
- Quality control test records
- Packaging records
- Storage and shipping records

**Traceability Information:**
- Batch number assignment
- Production date and time
- Raw material batch numbers
- Processing parameters
- Quality control results
- Packaging details

#### 6.2 Document Control System
**Document Management:**
- Unique document identification
- Version control procedures
- Approval and authorization requirements
- Distribution and access controls
- Archival and retention procedures

**Electronic Systems:**
- Digital document storage
- Backup and recovery procedures
- Access logging and audit trails
- Integration with ERP systems
- Electronic signature capabilities

### 7. EMERGENCY AND INCIDENT DOCUMENTATION

#### 7.1 Incident Report Form
**Required Information:**
- Incident date, time, and location
- Description of incident
- Immediate actions taken
- Injuries or damages (if any)
- Witness statements
- Corrective actions planned

**Reporting Requirements:**
- Immediate verbal notification
- Written incident report within 24 hours
- Follow-up reports as required
- Regulatory reporting (if applicable)

#### 7.2 Emergency Response Documentation
**Required Documents:**
- Emergency contact lists
- Spill response procedures
- First aid procedures
- Emergency equipment locations
- External emergency services contacts

**Training Records:**
- Emergency response training records
- Drill participation records
- Competency assessments
- Refresher training schedules

### 8. DOCUMENTATION STANDARDS AND FORMATS

#### 8.1 Document Format Standards
**General Requirements:**
- A4 paper size (210mm x 297mm)
- Company letterhead and logo
- Clear, legible printing
- Sequential numbering
- Date and version information

**Electronic Formats:**
- PDF format for archival documents
- Excel/CSV for data exports
- Secure email transmission
- Digital signature compatibility

#### 8.2 Language Requirements
**Primary Language:** English
**Additional Languages:** As required by destination country
**Technical Terms:** Industry standard terminology
**Clarity Requirements:** Unambiguous technical language

### 9. DOCUMENT RETENTION AND ARCHIVAL

#### 9.1 Retention Periods
**Commercial Documents:**
- Invoices and payment records: 7 years
- Purchase orders and contracts: 7 years
- Delivery notes and receipts: 5 years

**Quality Documents:**
- Certificates of Analysis: 5 years
- Quality control records: 5 years
- Batch records: 5 years

**Regulatory Documents:**
- Customs declarations: 7 years
- Hazardous goods documentation: 5 years
- Safety data sheets: Current version + 5 years

#### 9.2 Archival Procedures
**Physical Storage:**
- Climate controlled storage
- Fire resistant cabinets
- Organized filing systems
- Access control procedures

**Electronic Archival:**
- Secure digital storage
- Regular backup procedures
- Version control systems
- Disaster recovery plans

### 10. DOCUMENT SECURITY AND CONFIDENTIALITY

#### 10.1 Security Measures
**Physical Security:**
- Locked storage facilities
- Access control systems
- Visitor management procedures
- Document tracking systems

**Electronic Security:**
- Password protection
- Encryption requirements
- Access logging
- Anti-virus protection

#### 10.2 Confidentiality Requirements
**Sensitive Information:**
- Pricing information
- Proprietary formulations
- Business confidential data
- Personal information protection

**Handling Procedures:**
- Authorized access only
- Non-disclosure agreements
- Secure transmission methods
- Destruction procedures
