### 1. GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

#### 1.1 Scope
This appendix contains detailed product specification sheets for all products/materials to be supplied under this Purchase Order. All products must comply with these specifications and relevant international standards.

- **[Industry Standard Organization]** classifications

- **ISO (International Organization for Standardization)** specifications
- **OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer)** approvals
- **REACH (Registration, Evaluation, Authorization and Restriction of Chemicals)** compliance

### 6. QUALITY ASSURANCE

#### 6.1 Certificate of Analysis
Each batch of products/materials supplied must be accompanied by a Certificate of Analysis containing:
- Batch number and date of manufacture
- Test results for all specified parameters
- Compliance with specifications
- Shelf life and storage conditions
- Safety data sheet reference

#### 6.2 Quality Control Testing
**Supplier Testing Requirements:**
- Raw material testing
- In-process testing
- Finished product testing
- Stability testing
- Performance testing

**Independent Testing:**
- Third-party laboratory verification
- OEM approval testing
- Field performance monitoring
- Customer feedback analysis

### 7. STORAGE AND HANDLING

#### 7.1 Storage Conditions
- Temperature: 5°C to 40°C
- Humidity: Maximum 80% RH
- Protection from direct sunlight
- Clean, dry storage areas
- Separate storage for different products
