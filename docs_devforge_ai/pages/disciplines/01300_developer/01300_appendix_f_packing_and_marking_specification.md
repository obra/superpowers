# APPENDIX F: PACKING AND MARKING SPECIFICATION
## Product/Material Supply - Packing and Marking Specification

### 1. GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

#### 1.1 Scope
This appendix specifies the packing and marking requirements for all products/materials supplied under this Purchase Order. All packaging must ensure product integrity, safety, and compliance with transportation regulations. <!-- data-field="packaging_scope" data-ai-placeholder="PACKAGING_SCOPE" -->

#### 1.2 Packaging Standards
All packaging must comply with:
- **International standards** (ISO, ASTM, API)
- **Transportation regulations** (ADR, IMDG, IATA)
- **Environmental standards** (recycling, disposal)
- **Company packaging specifications**
- **Customer handling requirements**

### 2. PRIMARY PACKAGING REQUIREMENTS

#### 2.1 Steel Drums (205L)
**Technical Specifications:**
- Material: Cold-rolled steel, tin-plated interior
- Capacity: 205 liters nominal
- Wall thickness: 0.8mm minimum
- Internal coating: Epoxy phenolic or equivalent
- UN certification: Required for hazardous materials

**Construction Requirements:**
- Seamless or welded construction
- Pressure tested to 0.5 bar
- Leak-proof design
- Stackable up to 3 high when full
- Forklift and pallet jack accessible

**Quality Standards:**
- Clean and dry interior
- No contamination from previous contents
- Proper sealing with bungs and gaskets
- Pressure relief devices where required

#### 2.2 Plastic Bottles (1L, 4L, 5L)
**Technical Specifications:**
- Material: High-density polyethylene (HDPE)
- UV stabilization: Required for outdoor storage
- Chemical resistance: Compatible with lubricant formulations
- Wall thickness: 2mm minimum
- Tamper-evident caps: Required

**Design Requirements:**
- Ergonomic shape for handling
- Wide neck for easy filling and dispensing
- Graduated markings for measurement
- Child-resistant closures for retail products
- Recyclable material

#### 2.3 Plastic Pails (20L, 25L)
**Technical Specifications:**
- Material: HDPE with UV stabilization
- Capacity: 20L or 25L nominal
- Wall thickness: 3mm minimum
- UN certification: Required for hazardous materials
- Handle design: Reinforced plastic handles

**Construction Features:**
- Blow-molded construction
- Tamper-evident seals
- Pouring spout with cap
- Stackable design
- Recyclable material

#### 2.4 Grease Cartridges (400g)
**Technical Specifications:**
- Material: Aluminum or composite construction
- Capacity: 400g nominal
- Thread type: Standard grease gun fitting
- Seal integrity: Tamper-evident design
- Shelf life: 3 years minimum

**Design Requirements:**
- Easy loading into grease guns
- Clean dispensing
- No leakage during storage
- Recyclable components

### 3. SECONDARY PACKAGING REQUIREMENTS

#### 3.1 Pallet Configuration
**Standard Pallet Specifications:**
- Type: EUR/EPAL pallet (1200mm x 800mm)
- Load capacity: 1500kg minimum
- Material: Heat-treated wood
- ISPM 15 compliant: Required for international shipping

**Pallet Load Configurations:**
- Steel drums: 4 drums per pallet (2x2 configuration)
- Plastic pails: 36 x 20L pails per pallet
- Plastic bottles: 48 x 5L bottles per pallet
- Grease cartridges: 120 cartridges per pallet (6x20 configuration)

#### 3.2 Stretch Wrapping
**Requirements:**
- Material: LLDPE stretch film
- Thickness: 20-25 micron
- Pre-stretch ratio: 200-300%
- Color: Clear or black
- UV stabilization: Required

**Wrapping Specifications:**
- Complete pallet coverage
- 4-6 layers minimum
- Secure base layer
- Weather protection
- Load stability

#### 3.3 Protective Packaging
**Corner Protectors:**
- Material: Cardboard or plastic
- Thickness: 5mm minimum
- Size: 50mm x 50mm x pallet height
- Quantity: 4 per pallet

**Top Covers:**
- Material: Corrugated cardboard or plastic sheet
- Weather resistant: Required
- Secure attachment: Stretch wrapped
- Product identification: Visible through cover

- Material: Adhesive vinyl or polyester

#### 4.2 Secondary Packaging Labels
**Pallet Labels:**

**Label Specifications:**

#### 4.3 Hazard Labels and Placards

### 5. PACKAGING TESTING AND QUALITY CONTROL

#### 5.1 Packaging Integrity Testing
**Drop Testing:**
- Height: 1.2m for individual containers
- Surface: Concrete floor
- Orientation: Most vulnerable position
- Acceptance: No leakage or damage

**Stack Testing:**
- Duration: 24 hours minimum
- Load: 3 times pallet weight
- Temperature: 40°C
- Acceptance: No deformation or leakage

**Vibration Testing:**
- Frequency: 5-200 Hz
- Duration: 1 hour per axis
- Amplitude: 0.5mm
- Acceptance: No leakage or damage

#### 5.2 Quality Control Procedures
**Incoming Inspection:**
- Raw material verification
- Manufacturing quality checks
- Dimensional verification
- Material compatibility testing

**In-Process Inspection:**
- Filling accuracy verification
- Seal integrity testing
- Label application checks
- Packaging completeness verification

**Final Inspection:**
- Complete pallet inspection
- Documentation verification
- Sample retention
- Release authorization

### 6. STORAGE AND HANDLING REQUIREMENTS

#### 6.1 Packaging Storage Conditions
**Temperature Requirements:**
- Drums and pails: 5°C to 40°C
- Plastic bottles: 5°C to 35°C
- Grease cartridges: 5°C to 25°C

**Humidity Control:**
- Maximum 80% relative humidity
- Protection from condensation
- Dry storage areas

**Light Protection:**
- UV protection required
- Indoor storage preferred
- Covered outdoor storage allowed with protection

#### 6.2 Handling Procedures
**Manual Handling:**
- Weight limits: 25kg maximum per person
- Lifting equipment: Required for heavier loads
- Team lifting: For loads over 50kg
- Proper lifting techniques

**Mechanical Handling:**
- Forklift certification required
- Load stability verification
- Speed limits in storage areas
- Operator training requirements
