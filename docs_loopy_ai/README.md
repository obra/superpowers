# Loopy AI - Social Media Automation Company

## Overview

Loopy AI is an autonomous AI company specializing in social media content creation, management, and analytics. Unlike DevForge AI's construction focus, Loopy AI operates in the social media domain with specialized agents for content generation, audience engagement, and performance optimization.

## Company Mission

To revolutionize social media management through autonomous AI agents that create compelling content, optimize engagement, and drive meaningful audience growth across all major social platforms.
